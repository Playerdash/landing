FAQs

Everything you need to know about the product and how to get started. Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Please chat to our friendly team.

What kind of content can I share on Playerdash?

You can share videos, PDFs, images, website links, and analysis data — whatever your team needs. You can even integrate third-party survey or forms (e.g. google) on your page.

Does Playerdash work with my analysis software?

Yes! We support XML data from all major analysis tools. Just drag and drop your XML file along with the video to work with your data online and share it with your players.

How do athletes access the content I share?

You’ll have your own customizable Team Dashboard. Upload or link any content you want your athletes to access — for them, it’s just one click to find everything they need.

Can I share content with individual players?

Absolutely. You can choose whether content is visible to the whole team, just one player, or a specific group.

Can I try it for free?

Yes, you can test the full platform with no restrictions for at least 30 days. Just contact us — we’ll get you started with a short Zoom call.
