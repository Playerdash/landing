Mannheim Ladies Team qualifies again for the 2026 EHL season

Bojan
Updated on June 4, 2025July 17, 2025
Share:
  • Share this on WhatsApp
  • Share this on FaceBook
  • Share this on Twitter

The Mannheimer HC Ladies have secured their spot in the Euro Hockey League for a second consecutive year, following another dominant Bundesliga season ending the regular season at spot #1. After earning silver in their EHL debut in 2024, Mannheim returns to Europe’s top stage, underlining their status as a German powerhouse. Since 2021, the team has relied on Playerdash as their central team platform, supporting their rise both nationally and internationally. With back-to-back domestic titles and growing EHL experience, MHC continues to set the standard in German women’s hockey.
